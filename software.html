what is Software Engineering?
Software engineering is the process of designing, creating, testing, and maintaining software using systematic and disciplined methods. It involves the application of engineering principles and practices to the development of software, including the use of tools and techniques to manage the entire software development life cycle. The goal of software engineering is to develop high-quality software that meets the needs of users and stakeholders, while also being efficient, reliable, maintainable, and scalable..
